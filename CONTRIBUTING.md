# Contributing

First off, thank you for considering a contribution! Whether you're fixing a typo in the README, have advice on accessibility, or are a Quarto expert who can help me figure out commutative diagrams, your help is appreciated.

## 🗺️ How Can I Help?

### 1. Share Your Ideas
If you have a workflow idea or a stylistic suggestion, start a thread in the **Discussions** tab.

### 2. Report Bugs
If you encounter problems with the code, it's almost certainly my fault. Please open an **Issue**. Try to include:
* Your operating system.
* The specific command you ran.
* A small snippet of the `.qmd` file that caused the trouble.

### 3. Improve the Logic
If you can improve my Lua filters or have ideas for `chopper.py`, please feel free to open a pull request. Ensure contributions render correctly in both **HTML** and **PDF** formats. Please keep Lua filters documented with comments---some of us (including the maintainer!) are still learning! Be sure to test both student- and instructor-facing builds.
